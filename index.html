Smart Aquarium

"Smart Aquarium" is an innovative IoT project tailored for aquarium enthusiasts. It introduces automation by providing automatic food feeding and real-time monitoring of water quality. This project serves the crucial purpose of ensuring the optimal health and well-being of aquatic pets. With features like automated feeding, users can maintain a consistent and balanced diet for their fish. Real-time water quality monitoring offers timely insights, enabling proactive measures to sustain a healthy aquatic environment. This user-friendly system not only simplifies aquarium maintenance but also enhances the overall experience of caring for aquatic pets, making it an essential tool for enthusiasts and pet owners alike.

Appointment Scheduler

"Appointment Scheduler" is an intuitive and user-friendly system designed for seamless appointment booking. Users can effortlessly schedule appointments, considering the availability of doctors, streamlining the booking process. The system ensures user convenience by sending email notifications, keeping users well-informed about their scheduled appointments. This efficient scheduling tool not only simplifies the booking process but also enhances communication by providing timely reminders. With its user-centric design, the Appointment Scheduler aims to optimize the scheduling experience, making it an indispensable solution for both users and healthcare professionals.
